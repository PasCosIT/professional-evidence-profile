How it works

Explainable analysis for evidence-backed professional profiles

EviLayer provides an explainable analysis focused on professional evidence, demonstrated capability signals, confidence level, evidence coverage, and source attribution from AI-assisted work.

What we collect

  • AI export ZIP files or conversations.json.
  • Conversation titles, dates, and messages.
  • Message author: user, assistant, or unknown.
  • Professional, temporal, and technical signals extracted from approved text.

What we exclude or protect

  • Personal, sensitive, or uncertain conversations are excluded by default.
  • Emails, phone numbers, tokens, passwords, and names are redacted.
  • Copied third-party content is not automatically attributed to the user.
  • Original conversations are not exposed in the public profile.

Generated outputs

  • Data scan with categories and excluded content.
  • Manual review before analysis.
  • EviLayer Snapshot supported by evidence.
  • EviLayer Evidence Report with Evidence Appendix.

Your evidence. Your control.

EviLayer analyses only the professional evidence intentionally provided by the user. Personal and sensitive content should be excluded before the profile is generated. Every evidence item can be reviewed, removed or marked as uncertain.

    Prototype API endpoints

    • POST /api/import: upload and normalize file.
    • POST /api/analyze: generate insights, radar, timeline, and Technology Reasoning.
    • POST /api/report: regenerate reports after visibility decisions.
    • POST /api/delete: remove the in-memory session.

    MVP privacy principles

    • Only approved professional content.
    • Local redaction before analysis.
    • No external APIs in this MVP.
    • Original conversations are not shown in the public profile.
